Key features of our company

Psychiatric evaluation

This involves a comprehensive assessment of a patient's mental health condition, including their medical history, symptoms, and current mental state.

Individual therapy

Mental health hospitals can offer individual therapy to patients. This can involve a range of approaches, including cognitive-behavioral therapy, psychodynamic therapy, and supportive therapy.

Group therapy

Mental health hospitals may offer group therapy sessions, which can provide patients with a supportive environment where they can discuss their feelings and experiences with others who are going through similar struggles.

Electroconvulsive therapy (ECT)

Mental health hospitals may offer ECT for patients with severe depression or other mental health conditions that have not responded to other forms of treatment.
